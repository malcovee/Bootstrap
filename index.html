Japan

Japan is a global leader in the automotive industry, famous for its reliable, fuel-efficient, and technologically advanced cars.

Some of the most popular Japanese car brands include Toyota, Honda, Nissan, Mazda, Suzuki, Subaru, Mitsubishi, Lexus (luxury by Toyota), Acura (luxury by Honda), and Infiniti (luxury by Nissan).

London

London isn't known for a specific type of car like some cities known for their car manufacturers.

London has a well-developed public transport system https://centreforlondon.org/blog/car-ownership-census/, so car ownership is lower than the UK average. Many residents find it more convenient and affordable to get around without a car.

Brazil

Brazil boasts a major automotive industry, ranking 8th in the world for annual vehicle production.

Many international brands have factories in Brazil, including General Motors, Volkswagen, Fiat, Toyota, and Renault.

Dubai

Dubai's car scene is all about luxury, performance, and reflecting a flashy lifestyle.

Luxury Reigns Supreme: Dubai is known for its wealth, and that's reflected in the cars. Brands like Bugatti, Ferrari, Lamborghini, Rolls Royce, and Bentley are common sights.
